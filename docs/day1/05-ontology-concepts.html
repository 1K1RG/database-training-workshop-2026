Session Overview

Concepts, vocabulary, and structure of biological ontologies as used in plant genomics databases. This lecture provides the conceptual grounding needed before the Chado schema deep dive that follows.

Topics Covered (Placeholder)

  • What is an ontology and why databases use them
  • Key ontologies in plant biology: Gene Ontology (GO), Plant Ontology (PO), Crop Ontology (CO)
  • Terms, relationships, and hierarchy
  • Controlled vocabulary vs. free text
  • How ontology terms anchor traits in SNP-Seek
  • Practical examples from rice phenotyping data
